BIRDS SPECIES CLASSIFICATION



Bird species refer to the different types or kinds of birds that exist in the world. There are thousands of bird species, each with its own unique characteristics, behaviors, and habitats. Birds are classified into various species based on their physical attributes, such as size, shape, coloration, beak structure, and plumage patterns, as well as their genetic makeup and evolutionary history.
